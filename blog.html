Blog 4: Future Planning and Certifications

I recently met with my advisor about my situation and we brainstormed a few plans to move forward. I originally planned to graduate with an AAS in programming but seeing how my role at the bike shop has played out, I decided to look a bit into networking and cybersecurity programs and certificates.

I found out FRCC has certificate programs that not only provide certificates through FRCC, but correlate with relavant CompTIA certificate's that I could also obtain in the process. It also appears that the CompTIA certification itself counts as credit for the class as well as the FRCC certificate. In learning this, I decided to focus this summer on obtaining the first CompTIA certification on my own since I already have a fair amount of knowledge of the A+ certification topics. Then I'll take the rest of the certification classes, and be able to graduate with a programming degree, but also a few certifications in networking and job experience to back it up.
