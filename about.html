About Me

// background & interests

I'm a Computer Science student at Front Range Community College, graduating in May 2026 with an Associate of Science in Computer Science and a 20-credit Programming Certificate. I'm based in Boulder, Colorado, where I also work as an IT Developer and System Administrator at University Bicycles.

I've been programming since high school — self-taught, following whatever seemed interesting at the time. That's taken me through game development and scripting, into OS internals, compiler and assembler design, and systems programming in C and C++. I'm most comfortable working close to the hardware, and I spend most of my time on Linux.

My current technical interests are low-level systems programming, computer architecture, cybersecurity, and open-source tooling. I'm also drawn to the intersection of infrastructure and software — build systems, system administration, networked services. I'm actively working toward CompTIA certifications alongside my degree.

Outside of programming I'm a Colorado native who skateboards, bikes, and gets into the mountains when I can. I also volunteer at Community Cycles Co-op in Boulder, helping refurbish donated bikes for the community.
